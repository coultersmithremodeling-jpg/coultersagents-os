Privacy & Security

Your data is yours. Period.

🔒 End-to-End Encryption

All data stored in the Vault is encrypted with AES-256. Your integration credentials are never stored in plaintext. We use Supabase Row Level Security to ensure only you can access your data.

🚫 No Data Selling

We will never sell, share, or monetize your personal data. Your emails, messages, and financial data remain strictly yours.

📋 SOC 2 Compliant

Our infrastructure meets SOC 2 Type II standards. Regular security audits and penetration testing ensure your data stays safe.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Request full data deletion at any time from Settings. We'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days.
